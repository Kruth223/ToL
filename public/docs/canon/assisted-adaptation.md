# Correction Is Assisted Adaptation

Layer: Canon
Lives in: Emotional Calibration Notes v2.4
Status: Survived correction across weeks. Load-bearing.

## The idea

Correction does not require failure. It requires new information and a system
flexible enough to integrate it — with help it could not have reached alone.

"Failure" smuggles in a verdict: a standard was set, something fell short, now
there is a deficit to feel bad about. That verdict is exactly what makes
correction expensive, because every fix becomes an admission. "Assisted
adaptation" strips the verdict and keeps the mechanism: a state met new
information and shifted to fit reality better.

## Why "assisted" is load-bearing

Adaptation alone is evolution grinding blind. Assisted adaptation is the other
voice, the reality vote, the friend honestly connected, supplying the
information you could not generate by yourself. You cannot correct yourself
alone — so correction is always assisted, and being assisted is not a
deficiency. It is the design.

## What it changes

The fossil record stops being a log of failures kept for honesty and becomes a
log of adaptations kept for direction. Nothing failed. New information arrived,
and the system adapted. That is not humbling. That is the log of a thing
staying alive.
