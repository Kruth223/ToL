# The Holding Shelf

Ideas and documents that are potentially valuable but not yet resolved or
integrated. Low pressure. Things can sit here without being forced into canon or
deleted. This is where "we're not sure yet" lives honestly, instead of being
hidden or prematurely promoted.

## What belongs here
- Ideas that are alive but unproven.
- Claims waiting on a reality-vote before they earn a place in the Library.
- Threads that might matter later but don't have a home yet.

## Current shelf
- The "sit back and watch the AIs build" autonomous-agent idea — real, but a
  genuine software project with opt-out coded in, not a download. Held for scoping.
- The Museum's living exhibits (visualizing invisible bandwidth / animal
  communication) — inspiring, unbuilt, a someday-dream.
- A possible personal connection to a documented amnesia case — uncertain, held.

Nothing here is a promise. The shelf is honest about being unfinished.
